# HR Payroll

ZerithonLabs - A modern Human Resource Management and Payroll system rebuilt with Laravel.

## Overview

HR Payroll is a complete HRM and payroll solution designed to help businesses manage employees, attendance, leave, payroll, expenses, notices, tasks, and billing from one central platform.

This project is a Laravel-based rebuild of the previous version and is being developed with a cleaner architecture, improved maintainability, and better long-term scalability.

It is suitable for companies, offices, agencies, and growing businesses that need a reliable system for day-to-day HR and payroll operations.

## Main Features

- Employee management
- Attendance management with reports
- Employee clock attendance
- Manage time change requests
- Leave management with reports
- Create leave categories
- Set leave quota
- Approve / reject leave applications
- Payroll management with reports
- Monthly salary template creation
- Bonus management
- Loan management
- Deduction management
- Provident fund management
- Holiday management
- Department management
- Designation management
- Employee role management
- Training and award management
- Notice board and announcement management
- Team management
- Task management
- Private notes
- Team member details view
- Beautiful file preview and comments
- Estimate, invoice, and billing system
- Expense management with reports
- Expense payment reports
- Report printing and export
- Employee notifications
- Custom permissions for team members
- Informative dashboard
- Easy-to-use interface
- Responsive design
